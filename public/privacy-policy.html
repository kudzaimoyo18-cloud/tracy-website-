Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

Wango Marketing (“Wango Marketing”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is a growth marketing agency based in Business Bay, Dubai, United Arab Emirates. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal data when you visit our website, contact us, or engage our services. By using our website you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly — such as your name, email address, phone number, company name, budget range, and the contents of any message you send through our contact form or by email, phone, or social media.
  • Information collected automatically — such as your IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, referring URLs, and interaction data, gathered through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Information from third parties — such as analytics providers, advertising platforms, and social media networks where you interact with our content.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal data we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide the services you request;
  • Prepare proposals, quotes, and strategy recommendations;
  • Operate, maintain, and improve our website and services;
  • Send service updates and, where you have opted in, marketing communications;
  • Measure the performance of our marketing and advertising campaigns;
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud or misuse.

3. Legal Bases for Processing

Where applicable law requires it, we process your personal data on the basis of your consent, the performance of a contract with you, our legitimate business interests, and/or compliance with a legal obligation.

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the site, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors use our website. We may use third-party analytics and advertising tools (for example, web analytics and social media pixels) that set their own cookies. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function properly as a result.

5. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with trusted service providers who perform functions on our behalf (such as hosting, analytics, email delivery, and advertising), with professional advisers, with authorities where required by law, or in connection with a business transfer such as a merger or acquisition. These parties are only permitted to use your data as necessary to provide their services to us.

6. International Transfers

We are based in the United Arab Emirates and may process and store your data on servers located in other countries. Where we transfer data internationally, we take steps to ensure it receives an appropriate level of protection.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes set out in this policy, to comply with our legal and contractual obligations, and to resolve disputes. When data is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymise it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, update, or delete your personal data, to object to or restrict certain processing, to withdraw consent, and to request a copy of your data. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below. We will respond in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the UAE Personal Data Protection Law and, where relevant, the GDPR.

9. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures designed to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website and communications may contain links to third-party websites and social media platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. Material changes will be posted on this page.
